If you are a Coach or Athletic Director, thank you for stopping by. If you currently use the Amarillo Chapter we appreciate your support. We value the opportunity to furnish officials for you and your program. If there is anything that we can do for you, or if you have any questions, we hope that you will contact the appropriate individual for your concerns.

Coaches and Athletic Directors are able to view Varsity and Sub-varsity games assigned by the Amarillo Chapter to include assigned officials and many other features.

If you have not requested your Username and Password for on-line access to all your information, contact our Chapter Secretary

Current fee schedule for Varsity Games as prescribed by the University Interscholastic League may be view by clicking here. Mileage to your game site as furnished by the Texas Department of Public Safety may be viewed by clicking here.

If you are interested in the assigned crews for the current Football season, click Crews for a complete listing of all the crews. The list includes the Crew Chief, Referee, Umpire, Linesman, Line Judge and Back Judge.

Should you desire the Amarillo Chapter work your home schedule, contact the Chapter Secretary for information on submitting your schedule.

If you would like for an official from our Speakers Bureau to come to your school to discuss the current NCAA/Texas UIL football rule changes click on Request for Rules Clinic. We will be more than happy to provide an official to talk to your team or your Booster Club about the Rules of the Game.
